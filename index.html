    The world's leading academy for the royal game

    Hi and a warmly welcome to chessademy! We at chessademy have the aim to offer the best possible place for people who enjoy playing chess as well as for those who want to learn it or even master it and become a professional player! To achieve these goals we created different areas: The "playfield" where you always find somebody to play with. "academy", where you find hundrets of lessons with interactive exercises. And last but not least our "library" where you find articles, ebooks as well as videos about chess. Have fun discovering chessademy!
